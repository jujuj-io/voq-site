Welcome to Voq, the free text-to-speech Chrome extension that turns any webpage into an audio experience.

## What is Voq?

Voq is a Chrome extension that reads text aloud directly in your browser. Select any text, press play, and Voq reads it back to you. It works on articles, AI responses, forums, documentation — anywhere there is text.

## Why we built it

Reading on screens is tiring. Long articles, AI chat responses, forum threads — these take time to read and strain your eyes. Voq lets you listen while you do something else: commute, exercise, cook, or simply rest your eyes.

## What's next

We are working on more voice options, speed controls, and deeper integrations with the sites you use every day. Stay tuned.

In the meantime, [install Voq from the Chrome Web Store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/ckkihjnakpnccnmdipbdibeaibigjjde) and start listening.
